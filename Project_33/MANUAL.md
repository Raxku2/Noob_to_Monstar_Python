## Key Bindings & Commands

* `p`: **Play** the song currently in the queue.
* `u`: **Pause** the current song.
* `r`: **Resume** the paused song.
* `a`: **Stop** the current song and clear the queue.
* `pl`: **Pick & Play** a song from the list. Allows you to select a song by ID, add it to the queue, and start playing it immediately.
* `ls`: **List Songs** in your music folder. You can also add a song to the queue from this list.
* `Q`: **Quit** the player and stop any music currently playing.
